## 1. Creating a Customer Account

1. Open the Herufi app and tap **Create account**.
2. Fill in your details:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| Full Name | 2 or 3 words (e.g. "Amina Said Hassan") |
| Email | Your active email |
| Phone | Optional — select your country code, then type your local number |
| Role | Select **Customer — I want to shop and track orders** |
| Password | At least 8 characters, one uppercase, one digit, one symbol |

3. Tap **Create Account**. You are taken to the shop homepage.

> You can also sign up instantly with **Google**.

## 2. Browsing the Marketplace

After logging in, you land on the **Shop** page. Here you can:

- Browse all available products across stores
- Search by product name or keyword
- Filter by **category** (e.g. Food & Beverages, Electronics, Clothing)
- **Visual Search** — tap the camera icon and take a photo of any product to find it in the marketplace
- Sort by price or relevance

### Product Details

Tap any product to see:
- Full description
- Price (in TZS)
- Quantity available
- Shop name and location
- Similar products

## 3. Adding to Cart & Checkout

### Add to Cart

Tap **Add to Cart** on any product. The cart icon in the top bar shows how many items you have.

### Updating the Cart

Tap the **Cart** icon to open it. You can:
- Increase or decrease quantities
- Remove items
- See a breakdown of subtotal, tax, and total

### Checkout

1. Tap **Checkout**.
2. If items are from multiple shops, they are split into separate orders automatically.
3. Choose your **payment method**:
   - Cash on delivery
   - Mobile Money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money, etc.)
   - Card
   - Bank transfer
4. Choose your **purchase purpose**:
   - **Personal** — items are for yourself
   - **For my shop inventory** — if you are also a shop owner buying stock
5. Tap **Place Order**.

You will receive a confirmation and the order appears in your order history.

## 4. Tracking Your Orders

Go to your **Order History** in the shop section or tap on a specific order confirmation.

### Order Statuses

| Status | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Pending | Order placed, shop is reviewing it |
| Confirmed | Shop confirmed your order |
| Processing | Your order is being prepared |
| Shipped | Your order is on its way |
| Delivered | You have received your order |
| Cancelled | Order was cancelled |

### Getting Help With an Order

If you have an issue with an order (wrong item, damage, delay), contact the shop directly using the contact information on the shop's profile.

## 5. Loyalty Points

You earn **loyalty points** every time you make a purchase from a Herufi shop.

### How Points Work

- **1 point** earned for every **TZS 1,000** spent
- Points accumulate across all your purchases
- Your points balance is visible on your profile

### Redeeming Points

Shops may offer discounts or free products in exchange for loyalty points. Check with individual shops for their rewards.

## 6. AI Shopping Assistant

Herufi includes a built-in AI assistant that knows what's available in the marketplace.

### What It Can Do

- Help you find specific products ("I need 5kg of basmati rice")
- Compare products and prices
- Suggest alternatives if something is out of stock
- Remember your past purchases and make personal recommendations
- Answer questions in **English or Swahili**

### How to Access

Tap the **AI** icon or the chat bubble in the shop interface. You can have a full conversation — the assistant remembers your previous questions.

**Example questions:**
- "Do you have cooking oil in 5-litre containers?"
- "What's the cheapest option for sugar?"
- "Ninahitaji mchele kilo 10 — bei gani?" *(Swahili: I need 10kg of rice — what's the price?)*

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I shop from multiple shops in one order?**
A: Yes. Items from different shops are automatically split into separate orders at checkout, each processed by the respective shop.

**Q: How do I cancel an order?**
A: Contact the shop before the order is marked as "Processing". Once processing begins, cancellation may not be possible.

**Q: My order shows "Pending" for a long time — what do I do?**
A: Shops typically confirm orders within a few hours. If there is no update after 24 hours, use the shop's contact information to follow up.

**Q: Can I use Herufi as both a Customer and a Shop Owner?**
A: Yes. Sign up as a Shop Owner. From the **Choose Workspace** screen after login, you can enter your shop's dashboard or shop as a customer from the marketplace.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Herufi does not store card or mobile money credentials. All payment processing is handled by the respective payment providers.

**Q: How do I change the app language?**
A: Herufi supports English and Swahili. Change the language in **Settings** (available once you access the dashboard) or from the language toggle on the shop page.
